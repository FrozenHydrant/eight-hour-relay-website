Registration

    The Team Captain must register the team before individual runners can join.
  • Each team consists of 8 runners.
  • Each runner must complete their own individual registration.
  • Each runner must personally review and accept the Event Waiver.
  • The Team Captain cannot accept the Waiver on behalf of other runners.
  • If the Team Captain will participate as one of the 8 runners, the Captain must also complete an individual runner registration.
  • Only the Team Captain pays the team registration fee.
  • Individual runners do not make a separate registration payment.
  • A runner cannot join a team without a valid Team Code.
