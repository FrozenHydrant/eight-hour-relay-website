About the Race

The BMAI VANCOUVER RUNAHOLICS 8-Hour Relay is a team endurance event designed for runners of all levels. Unlike a traditional road race, this event focuses on teamwork, pacing, strategy, and community spirit. Each team consists of 8 runners. Runners take turns completing 30-minute legs in a fixed order. After all 8 runners complete their first leg, the same order repeats for the second round.

The goal is simple: cover the longest total distance within 8 hours. Hosted by Vancouver Runaholics Running & Fitness Society.

Basic Rules

  • Each team must have exactly 8 registered runners.
  • Each runner completes exactly two legs.
  • Each leg is approximately 30 minutes.
  • A ±3-minute margin is allowed to complete a full lap before exchange.
  • Teams must submit a fixed running order before the registration deadline.
  • The same running order is used for both rounds.
  • The team covering the greatest total distance within 8 hours wins.
  • Only completed laps are counted.

Divisions

Open Division
  • 15+ years old
  • No gender requirement
Mixed Division
  • 4 male and 4 female runners
  • 15+ years old
Master Division
  • All runners must be 50 years old or above.
  • Standard team: 4 male + 4 female.
  • Distance adjustment may apply if gender balance differs.

Registration Process

Each team registration requires the following information:

  • Team name
  • Division
  • Team Captain's information

Afterwards, runners join with the Team Code that is provided after team registration. Each runner will need to provide the following information:

  • Runner name
  • Email address
  • Phone & emergency contact
  • Gender/age

Once the runners have joined, the captain can edit their running order and also make a payment.
